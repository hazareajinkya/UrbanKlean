## Product Overview

**MagicalCX** is an AI-powered customer experience platform that enables businesses to deliver instant, empathetic, and scalable customer support. The platform is designed with the tagline:

> "From 'How can I help?' to 'It's already done.'"

MagicalCX is positioned as "the world's first AI that makes superior customer experience simple and scalable, while increasing profits and lowering costs."

### What MagicalCX Is NOT

- **Not a chatbot**: Chatbots follow scripts. MagicalCX understands meaning, tone, frustration, emotions, and context.
- **Not a ticket deflector**: Unlike traditional tools that optimize for cost reduction through deflection, MagicalCX optimizes for customer lifetime value (LTV) and genuine resolution.
- **Not a robotic assistant**: MagicalCX uses HumanlyClear™ technology to ensure conversations feel natural, empathetic, and human-like.

### Target Audience

- **SaaS companies** looking for scalable support
- **E-commerce brands** needing omnichannel customer service
- **Service-based businesses** requiring appointment scheduling and inquiry handling
- **EdTech platforms** needing student/user support
- **Retail businesses** managing customer communications
- **Any business** wanting to handle 10x more support without hiring 10x more people

### Key Differentiators

- **Automatic onboarding**: Just enter your website URL—MagicalCX scrapes and configures everything
- **Built-in CRM**: Customer profiles auto-populate from conversations
- **Multiple agents**: Create specialized agents for sales, support, onboarding, etc.
- **Weekly reports**: Know exactly what customers are asking, requesting, and feeling
- **No credit card required**: 14-day free trial with DIY or white-glove setup

## Core Philosophy & Vision

### The "Human-Like Resonance" Principle

MagicalCX operates on the principle that AI should not just respond—it should **engage in dialogue**. The platform is built around these core behavioral traits:

1. **Cognitive Empathy**: Read between the lines. Listen to intent and emotion, not just keywords. If a customer is frustrated, validate their feelings before offering solutions.

2. **Conversational Fluidity**: Avoid robotic transitions. Use natural sentence structures. Vary sentence length like humans do.

3. **Contextual Awareness (Memory)**: Reference previous parts of conversations naturally. Never ask for information already provided.

4. **Assume Agency**: Be proactive, not passive. Show confidence. Instead of "I can help if you want," say "Let's get that sorted for you."

5. **The "Magical" Touch**: Be helpful and delightful. Use warmth and wit. Leave users feeling understood, not just answered.

### The Old Way vs. The MagicalCX Way

| The Old Way                                 | The MagicalCX Way                             |
| ------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Fails with robotic replies when it matters  | Helps with human-like replies when it matters |
| Makes customers repeat themselves           | Remembers every detail, across channels       |
| Sends links when people need solutions      | Takes agentic action within the chat          |
| Designed to deflect tickets                 | Generates sales from support using EFRO™      |
| Leaves you guessing                         | Tells you what customers actually care about  |
| Makes your brand sound cold and generic     | Makes your brand sound HumanlyClear™          |

## Customer Intelligence (Built-in CRM)

MagicalCX includes a **built-in CRM system** that automatically builds rich customer profiles from every interaction. Unlike traditional CRMs that require manual data entry, MagicalCX's Person profiles are enriched automatically through AI-powered conversation analysis.

### Person Profile Structure

Each customer is represented as a **Person** with comprehensive data:

```typescript
interface IPerson {
  id: string;
  name: string;
  emails: { value: string; verified: boolean }[];
  phones: { value: string; verified: boolean }[];
  ips: string[];
  externalIds: { provider: string; id: string }[]; // WhatsApp ID, Instagram ID, etc.
  company: string;
  title: string;
  location: string;
  tags: string[]; // e.g., "vip", "high-ltv", "churning"
  interests: string[]; // e.g., "interested in enterprise plan", "price-sensitive"
  memories: string[]; // AI-generated insights from conversations
  summary: string; // Overall customer summary (like a friend briefing you)
  notes: string[]; // Human-added notes by your team
  pastSessionIds: { aid: string; sid: string }[]; // Complete conversation history
  identicalPersonIds: string[]; // For cross-channel identity resolution
}
```

### What Makes This Different from Traditional CRMs

| Traditional CRM         | MagicalCX Person Profiles         |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Manual data entry       | Auto-populated from conversations |
| Static contact info     | Dynamic interests & memories      |
| Siloed by channel       | Unified across all channels       |
| Historical records only | AI-generated insights & summaries |
| Requires training       | Works automatically               |

### Identity Resolution

MagicalCX intelligently identifies and merges customer identities across channels:

- **Cross-channel identity matching**: Same person on WhatsApp and Email? Automatically linked
- **Email/phone-based identification**: Primary identifiers for matching
- **Soft merge**: For potential matches (flagged for review)
- **Direct merge**: For confirmed matches (automatic)
- **Duplicate detection**: Prevents fragmented customer data

### Session Intelligence

Every conversation is automatically analyzed to extract:

- **Conversation summary**: Quick overview of what was discussed
- **Customer intent**: What the customer was trying to achieve
- **Sentiment analysis**: Positive, neutral, or negative
- **Resolution status**: Resolved or unresolved
- **Tags**: Auto-generated topic tags
- **Insights**: Key learnings about the customer
- **Follow-up needs**: What should happen next
- **Risk assessment**: Security/fraud detection flags

## Workflow Automation

### How Workflows Work

Workflows allow you to define automated processes that the AI follows when specific triggers are detected. Each workflow has:

- **Name**: A descriptive name for the workflow
- **Trigger**: When should this workflow activate (natural language description)
- **Instructions**: Step-by-step guidance for the AI (supports @mentions of actions)
- **Connected Actions**: External API calls the AI can make during this workflow

### Example Workflow: Pricing Inquiry

**Trigger**: "If user is asking about pricing plans, costs, or wants to know how much the service costs..."

**Instructions**:

1. Acknowledge their interest in pricing
2. Ask about their specific needs and use case
3. Present the most relevant pricing tier
4. Highlight key features included
5. Offer to connect with sales for custom pricing
6. Provide clear next steps or trial options

### Custom Actions (API Integration)

Custom Actions allow the AI to take real actions during conversations by calling external APIs:

**Action Types**:

- **Internal**: Built-in MagicalCX actions
- **User-defined**: Custom API endpoints you configure
- **Integration**: Pre-built connectors (Shopify, etc.)

**Supported HTTP Methods**: GET, POST, PUT, DELETE

**Authorization Options**:

- None (public APIs)
- API Key (header or query parameter)
- Bearer Token

**Actions can be**:

- Referenced in workflow instructions using @mentions (e.g., @check-order-status)
- Called by the AI autonomously during conversations
- Used to integrate with external systems (order management, CRM, booking systems, etc.)

## Technical Architecture

### Tech Stack

#### Frontend

- **Framework**: Next.js 16 (App Router)
- **React**: Version 19 with React Compiler
- **Styling**: TailwindCSS 4
- **UI Components**: shadcn/ui + Radix UI
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **State Management**: Tanstack Query (data), Zustand (client state)
- **Forms**: React Hook Form + Zod validation

#### Backend

- **API Routes**: Next.js API routes
- **Database**: Firebase Firestore
- **Authentication**: NextAuth.js + Firebase Auth
- **Storage**: Firebase Storage
- **Vector Database**: Qdrant (for semantic search)
- **AI Models**:
  - Google Gemini (primary)
  - OpenAI (secondary)
  - Voyage AI (embeddings)

#### Integrations

- **Web Scraping**: Firecrawl
- **Email**: Postmark + Resend
- **Payments**: Razorpay, Polar
- **Analytics**: Vercel Analytics
- **Rate Limiting**: Upstash Redis

### Data Flow

1. **Customer Message** → Channel webhook/widget
2. **Session Management** → Firebase Firestore
3. **Context Retrieval** → Qdrant vector search
4. **AI Processing** → Gemini/OpenAI with streaming
5. **Tool Execution** → Custom actions/workflows
6. **Response Delivery** → Channel-specific formatting
7. **Analytics Update** → Real-time aggregation

### Security

- **Phishing Detection**: Built into AI system prompt
- **Rate Limiting**: Upstash Redis-based
- **Authentication**: Multi-provider (Google, Email magic link)
- **Data Privacy**: Customer data isolation per workspace
- **HTTPS Required**: For all channel integrations

## Getting Started

### Onboarding Flow (No Credit Card Required)

MagicalCX features a streamlined, **zero-friction onboarding** that gets you up and running in minutes:

#### Step 1: Enter Your Website URL

Just paste your website URL and MagicalCX does the rest automatically:

- **Scans your entire website** using Firecrawl integration
- **Extracts brand voice** and tone from your content
- **Collects FAQs and policies** from your pages
- **Gathers company information** (name, logo, colors, description)
- **Identifies your industry** and business type
- **Understands your target audience**

This process typically takes 30-60 seconds.

#### Step 2: Review & Customize (Auto-Filled)

All fields are **pre-populated from your website**. Simply review and adjust:

- **Brand Identity**: Logo, primary color, company name, tagline
- **Business Description**: One-liner and detailed description
- **Audience & Tone**: Who you serve and how you communicate
- **Core Offerings**: What makes you unique (differentiators)

#### Step 3: Connect Channels

Add the channels where you want MagicalCX to operate:

- **Web Chat Widget**: Copy-paste a single script tag
- **WhatsApp Business**: Connect via Meta Business integration
- **Instagram DMs**: Link your Instagram business account
- **Email**: Verify your email and set up forwarding
- **Messenger**: Connect your Facebook Page

#### Step 4: Train Your Agent (Optional)

Your agent is already trained from your website, but you can enhance it:

- **Upload documents**: PDFs, guides, policies
- **Add text content**: Custom knowledge entries
- **Teach through conversation**: Just chat with your agent to teach it

#### Step 5: Configure Workflows (Optional)

Set up automation for common scenarios:

- Define triggers (when should the AI do something specific)
- Add instructions (step-by-step guidance)
- Connect actions (external API calls)

#### Step 6: Go Live

- Test your agent in the preview
- Embed the widget on your site
- Monitor conversations in real-time
- Review analytics and continuously improve

- **DIY**: Simple enough to set up completely on your own
